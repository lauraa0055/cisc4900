Tutorial

Searching for Recipes

  • To search for a specific recipe you had in mind, click on the search bar and enter the recipe name. Upon clicking submit, the website will return
    search results based on the recipe name you entered. Click on a recipe card from the results to view its information.
  • To search for a recipe including ingredients you currently have, click on the search bar and enter the ingredients(using commas to separate each ingredient)
    A list of recipes containing the ingredients you have entered will be generated. Click on a recipe card from the results to view its information.

Navigating the Website

  • To view the entire index of recipes on the website, click the "Recipe List" button on the home page
  • To find tools to aide your cooking process, click the "Other" button on the home page. The website will take you to a page with a measurement converter
    and a timer.
  • To know more information about the website, click the "Logo" button on the home page
